About Me

I was born in Manhattan Beach, California, where I lived until I was 18. I grew up playing volleyball and soccer, and continued to play club volleyball at The University of Texas-Austin, where I attended college. As a student at UT-Austin I studied K-6 Education and European Studies before finally landing in the Radio-Television-Film department. I graduated in 2015 with a BS degree in Film, and have since worked on several sets. When not working on set, I work as a server and bartender. I've worked at several notable Austin restaurants, and enjoy the fast pace and customer interactions. Since the pandemic, I have been in a coding bootcamp and will graduate in December of 2020.

Earbud

This app is designed for the audiophile who would like to keep track of and review their favorite songs. Created in conjunction with Staci Shon, Isaac Arnold, and Julian Rivera.

Get Me Outta Here!

This app is designed to geolocate the user, provide them with options of what type of location they're looking to go to, and provide them with options and a map showing them where that destination is. It was made in conjunction with Satya Kavya and Ted Peters.

Password Generator

This project creates a randomly generated password between 8 and 128 characters based on the users desired parameters. Alerts pop up when the generate password is clicked that ask a user what type of characters they would like. When all conditions are met, a password appears.

Day Planner

This web day planner shows you a 9am-5pm day, with each time block displaying a different color based on if it's in the future, past, or present. When a user enters their task in time block and clicks save, that input is saved to local storage to refer to later.
