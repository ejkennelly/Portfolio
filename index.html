About Me

I was born in Manhattan Beach, California, where I lived until I was 18. I grew up playing volleyball and soccer, and continued to play club volleyball at The University of Texas-Austin, where I attended college. As a student at UT-Austin I studied K-6 Education and European studies before finally landing in the Radio-Television-Film department. I graduated in 2015 with a BS degree in Film, and have since worked on several sets. When not working on set, I work as a server and bartender. I've worked at several notable Austin restaurants, and enjoy the fast pace and customer interactions.


During the pandemic, I was furloughed from my serving job, and have since decided to start a coding bootcamp. I love puzzles and figuring out why and how things work, so this seemed like a natural fit, and so far, I love it! I look forward to the weeks ahead, and growing my portfolio.
